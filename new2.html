HUMANITY

There’s no question that elephants are high up in the hierarchy of animals in terms of size and majesty. These days, people are fortunate to be able to get up close and personal with these gigantic gentle creatures, either in amusement parks, zoos or safari tours. Some of them are caught from their natural environment and then bred selectively in order for their strongest and best traits to surface, in a way that makes humans happy. But, is a human being’s happiness more important than an animal’s?
